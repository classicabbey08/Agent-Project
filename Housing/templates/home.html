Frequently Asked

How do I know the listings are real? +
Every property is manually verified before going live. We require real photos taken on the day of listing, video walkthroughs, and landlord ID confirmation. If anything is fishy, it's flagged immediately.
Do I have to pay agent fees? +
No. NaijaNest connects you directly with landlords or verified agents. There are no hidden fees, no agency charges, and no "platform fees" for tenants. Listing is also free for landlords.
Can I ask questions before visiting? +
Yes — message landlords directly to ask about water supply, power (NEPA vs generator), security, parking, neighbours, and anything else. Only travel when you're confident it's the right place.
How do I list my property? +
Create a free account, click "List Property", fill in the details, upload real photos, and submit. Our team will review and publish it within 24 hours. It's completely free.
What areas of Lagos are covered? +
We currently cover all major areas including Lekki, Ikoyi, Victoria Island, Surulere, Ikeja, Ajah, Yaba, Magodo, Gbagada, and more. We're expanding every week.
