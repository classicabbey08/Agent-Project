Common Questions
How do I report a fake listing?
Use the "Report" button on any listing page, or send us a message through this contact form with the listing link and details. We investigate all reports within 24 hours.
How long does it take to verify my listing?
We review and verify all new listings within 24 hours of submission. You'll receive a notification once your listing is approved and live.
Can I list multiple properties?
Yes, you can list as many properties as you own or manage. Each property goes through our verification process individually.
Is NaijaNest free to use?
Yes — it is completely free for both tenants and landlords. Tenants pay no fees to browse or contact landlords, and landlords pay nothing to list their properties.
